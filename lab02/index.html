Banana Bread Recipe

Quick and Easy Homemade Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350° and grease a loaf pan.
  2. Mash up the ripe bananas in a bowl.
  3. In a bowl, mix the bananas, eggs, butter, sugar, and vanilla extract together.
  4. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt to the batter and stir until mixed.
  5. Pour the batter into the loaf pan.
  6. Bake for about an hour, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the bread comes out clean.
  7. Allow the banana bread to cool down and enjoy!

This is my favorite recipe because it is really simple to make and the results are delicious!
