The Princess and the Frog Review

A Magical Story Set in the City of New Orleans

Main Actors

  • Anika Noni Rose as Tiana
  • Bruno Campos as Prince Naveen
  • Jennifer Cody as Charlotte
  • Keith Davis as Dr. Facilier

Favorite Songs

  1. "Almost There"
  2. "Friends on the Other Side"
  3. "Dig a Little Deeper"

The Princess and the Frog is an animated film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. The film centers around Tiana, a hardworking young lady who dreams of opening her own restaurant in New Orleans. Naveen is a prince who has been turned into a frog by Dr Facilier, an evil witch doctor. Naveen gives Tiana a kiss, thinking she is a princess, in an attempt to break the charm and transforms her into a frog as well. The two go on an adventure through the bayou to get turned back into humans before it is too late.
